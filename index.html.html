Peanut butter Jelly recipe

Julia Davis Chandler

    2 slices sandwich bread 2 tablespoons peanut butter 2 teaspoons grape jelly
    1. On one slice of bread, spread peanut butter evenly over the bread. 2.On the other slice of bread, spread the jelly evenly over the bread. 3.Put the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing in.
